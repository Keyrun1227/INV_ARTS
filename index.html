Welcome to ANU Arts Commerce & Law

ANU - College of Arts, Commerce, Law is a constituent College of Acharya Nagarjuna University as a pioneering institution with an aim of imparting arts values in the students who can change the shape of global scenario.

Our college provides best infra-structure facilities with best state-of-the- art technology. Committed and dedicated faculty with rich and diversified experience in teaching and research make learning experience in classroom highly influential and enriching. Students are motivated and guided by a strong team of faculty and technical staff who involved in them a positive value system that shapes their attitude, conduct and value system. Seminars, Workshops, Conferences, Tech fests and academic exhibitions are organized to upgrade knowledge and skills of our students.
